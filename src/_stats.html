Our domestic
consumption is
137 litres of water
everyday.

This is how we
manage those
137 litres

Bathing and Showering Flushing toilet Laundry Cooking and drinking Cleaning
Water for
Domestic Consumption
 137 litres


There is a little
problem though,
because what we
consume at home
is visible to us, but
it is only a small
bit of what we use
in total.
There are two
invisible parts.

The first invisible
part is the water
used for the
production of the
industrial products
we consume
everyday, such as
paper, cotton,
clothes. This part
amounts to
167 litres per day.

The second big
invisible part is
associated with
the production of
the food we
consume.
This amounts to
3496 litres per day
which means that
92% of the water
we use is invisible
and it is hidden in our food!
